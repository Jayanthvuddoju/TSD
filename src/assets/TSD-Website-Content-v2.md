## SOLUTION 3 — CYBERSECURITY

**Heading:** Cybersecurity

**Body:**
Cyber threats are growing more sophisticated, frequent, and costly every year. A single breach can cost millions in losses, regulatory penalties, and reputational damage. TSD helps organizations build a security posture that makes them a hard target — not just on paper, but in practice.

From initial risk assessments to ongoing security operations, we provide end-to-end cybersecurity services designed for the realities of today's threat landscape.

**Security Assessment & Auditing**
Comprehensive evaluation of your current security posture — identifying vulnerabilities, gaps, and risk exposure before attackers do.

**Penetration Testing**
Ethical hacking exercises that simulate real-world attack scenarios, testing your people, processes, and technology under controlled conditions.

**Identity & Access Management (IAM)**
Design and implement robust IAM frameworks to ensure the right people have the right access — and nothing more. Expertise in CyberArk, SailPoint, and Azure AD.

**Compliance Management**
Navigate complex regulatory requirements with expert guidance — from ISO 27001 and GDPR to Malaysia's PDPA and Singapore's MAS TRM.

**Security Operations Center (SOC)**
24/7 monitoring, detection, and response capabilities — either fully managed or as an augmentation to your in-house team.

**Incident Response & Recovery**
When a breach occurs, every minute counts. Our incident response team moves fast to contain, investigate, and recover — minimizing damage and downtime.

**Compliance Frameworks We Support:**
ISO 27001 · NIST CSF · GDPR · Malaysia PDPA · PCI-DSS · SOC 2 · MAS TRM (Singapore) · BNM RMiT (Malaysia)
